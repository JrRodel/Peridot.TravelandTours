Bohol Bliss

Arrival in Bohol

Day 1

Morning

  • Arrive at Tagbilaran Airport.
  • Meet and greet by Peridot Travel and Tours tour representatives
  • 9:00 AM - Transfer to the hotel

Afternoon

  • 12:00 PM - Check-in at Vest Grand Suites.
  • Lunch at a local eatery.
  • 2:00 PM - Countryside Tour.
  • Visit the Blood Compact Shrine.
  • Explore the Baclayon Church and Museum.
  • Take a scenic drive through the Mahogany Forest.

Evening

  • 6:00 PM - Dinner at a local restaurant.

Panglao Island Hopping

Day 2

Morning

  • 7:00 AM - Breakfast at the hotel.
  • 8:00 AM - Depart for Panglao Island Hopping.

Afternoon

  • Island-hopping tour includes visits to Balicasag Island for snorkeling, Virgin Island for a relaxing beach experience, and Pungtud Island.
  • Lunch included during the island-hopping tour.
  • Return to the hotel for a brief rest.

Evening

  • 6:00 PM - Dinner at the famous Alona Beach

Chocolate Hills Adventure

Day 3

Morning

  • 6:00 AM - Early breakfast at the hotel.
  • 7:00 AM - Depart for Chocolate Hills.

Late Morning

  • Explore the Chocolate Hills and take in the panoramic view.
  • Visit the Tarsier Sanctuary to see these adorable primates.

Afternoon

  • Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Visit the Butterfly Conservatory.

Evening

  • 6:30 PM - Dinner at the Loboc River Cruise.
  • 8:30 PM - Return to the hotel.

Departure

Afternoon

  • 3:00 PM - Departure.
